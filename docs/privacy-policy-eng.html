Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 10, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to FreeTravelApp! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application for travel and route management.

2. Information We Collect

  • Required: Email address for login and notifications.
  • Optional: Name, profile photo, route comments, and any content you choose to provide.
  • Bug Reports: May include optional screenshots and descriptions.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Authentication and account management.
  • Sending notifications about route requests and approvals.
  • Displaying ratings and comments.
  • Processing bug reports and improving the app.

4. Additional Information

  • Location Data: The app may process location-related information such as selected routes, starting and destination points.
  • User Content: Messages, comments, ratings, and other content shared within the app may be stored and displayed to other users.
  • Push Notifications: We may send notifications related to route requests, approvals, messages, and other app activities.
  • Content Moderation: Users can report other users or content. We review reports and take appropriate action when necessary.

Data Retention

We retain user data only for as long as necessary to provide the app's functionality. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.

GDPR Compliance

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data in accordance with GDPR.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to provide app functionality (e.g., storing screenshots securely).

6. Your Rights

  • Access your data stored in the app.
  • Edit or delete your profile.
  • Opt-out of notifications or emails.

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. Passwords are encrypted and sensitive data is stored securely.
